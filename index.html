About Us

Carmel Giri English Medium School, affiliated with the CBSE Board, provides high-quality education while fostering moral values, discipline, and cultural awareness. We are run by the Carmelite Missionaries of the Church (CMC) and were founded by the visionary, Saint Kuriakose Elias Chavara.

Our Mission & Values

At Carmel Giri, we believe in shaping our students into responsible citizens with strong moral values. We focus on:

  • Providing a nurturing and safe environment for learning
  • Fostering respect for cultural diversity
  • Instilling a sense of discipline and ethical values
  • Encouraging academic excellence and personal growth

Our Facilities

Our school is equipped with modern facilities that ensure the holistic development of our students:

  • Well-equipped classrooms with modern teaching aids
  • State-of-the-art science and computer labs
  • Spacious auditorium for cultural and educational events
  • Large playground for sports and recreational activities
  • Transportation services with special care for students
